Services

Thank you for your interest in PIX Mobile services! Walk-in customers are accepted. Please note that during peak hours, there may be a short wait. To minimize any wait times, we highly recommend scheduling appointments in advance online or by phone.
By requesting our services, customers agree to comply with our store policies, including respecting staff and fellow customers, adhering to appointment schedules, and understanding warranty terms.

Scheduling an appointment for a service is the best way to guarantee short wait times. Please make sure to double-check the location you are scheduling at!
